Professional Ethics Framework for AI Use

A systematic approach to ethical AI integration in professional practice:

Before AI Use
• Assess appropriateness for the task
• Evaluate potential risks and benefits
• Consider client and stakeholder impacts
• Review applicable policies and regulations
During AI Work
• Monitor outputs for quality and bias
• Maintain human oversight and judgment
• Document processes and decisions
• Protect confidential information
After AI Assistance
• Review and verify all outputs
• Provide appropriate attribution
• Assess outcomes for fairness
• Learn and improve processes
Ongoing Monitoring
• Regular ethical audits
• Bias detection and correction
• Policy updates and training
• Stakeholder feedback integration
