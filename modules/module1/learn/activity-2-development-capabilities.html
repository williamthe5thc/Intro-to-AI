Learning Activity 2: Understanding AI Development and Capabilities

OVERVIEW

This activity focuses on understanding how AI capabilities have developed over time and what makes current AI systems particularly relevant for working professionals.

Content Type: Interactive Video
Duration: 20 minutes

LEARNING SEGMENTS

Segment 1: From Rules to Learning (5 minutes)

  • Rule-based systems vs. machine learning
  • The shift toward data-driven AI
  • Why modern AI is more flexible

Segment 2: The Data Revolution (8 minutes)

  • How big data enabled AI breakthroughs
  • The role of the internet in AI training
  • Quality vs. quantity in AI training data

Segment 3: AI in Professional Contexts (7 minutes)

  • Email filtering and spam detection
  • Recommendation systems
  • Voice assistants and natural language processing
  • Document analysis and automation

Key Concepts

  • Machine Learning: Systems that improve through experience
  • Neural Networks: Computing systems inspired by biological brains
  • Natural Language Processing: AI's ability to understand human language
  • Automation: Using AI to perform routine tasks

Reflection Questions

  1. How has AI already impacted your daily work routine?
  2. What AI applications do you currently use without realizing it?
  3. Where do you see the biggest opportunities for AI in your profession?
