Module 1: History of AI for Working Professionals

Welcome to the first module of our journey into artificial intelligence! Discover how AI evolved from science fiction to an essential tool for today's working professionals.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Understand the origins and evolution of AI technology
  • Identify key milestones in AI development
  • Recognize the transition from academic research to practical applications
  • Appreciate the current state of AI in professional environments

Module Structure

  • Learning Activity 2: Understanding AI Development and Capabilities
  • Learning Activity 3: AI Applications in Professional Contexts

Practice - Apply Your Knowledge

Hands-on activities to reinforce your understanding:

  • Module 1 Matching Activity - Connect Historical Events

Assets - Test & Discuss

Assessments and collaborative learning opportunities:

  • History of AI Knowledge Check Quiz
  • Discussion: Historical Patterns and Future Implications

Why This Matters

Understanding AI's history gives you powerful insights:

  • Appreciate Current Capabilities: See how today's AI tools are the result of decades of innovation
  • Understand Limitations: Learn from past failures and overpromising to set realistic expectations
  • Make Informed Decisions: Use historical patterns to evaluate new AI technologies critically
  • Prepare for the Future: Understand the trajectory of AI development and its implications
