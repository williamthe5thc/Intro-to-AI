Module 1 Matching Activity

INSTRUCTIONS

This hands-on activity helps you apply your understanding of AI history by matching key developments with their time periods and significance.

Activity Type: Interactive Matching Exercise
Estimated Time: 15 minutes

PART A: TIMELINE MATCHING

Match each AI milestone with its approximate time period:

Milestones:

  • The Turing Test proposal
  • First neural network
  • Deep Blue defeats chess champion
  • GPT and large language models
  • Expert systems commercialization
  • Dartmouth AI Conference

Time Periods:

  • 1950s
  • 1960s
  • 1980s
  • 1990s
  • 2010s
  • 2020s

PART B: IMPACT ASSESSMENT

For each milestone, briefly describe (2-3 sentences) why it was significant for the development of AI as a tool for working professionals.

PART C: CURRENT APPLICATIONS

List 5 AI applications you encounter in your professional life and trace each back to a historical development from the timeline.

Example:

Email spam filtering → Traces back to early machine learning algorithms from the 1980s-1990s

Submission Guidelines

  • Complete all three parts of the activity
  • Submit your responses in the designated submission area
  • Be prepared to discuss your answers in the module discussion forum

Assessment Criteria

  • Accuracy of historical timeline matching (40%)
  • Quality of impact analysis (40%)
  • Relevance of current application examples (20%)
