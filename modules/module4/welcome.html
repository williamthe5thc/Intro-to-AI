Module 4: AI Media Creation for Working Professionals

Discover the creative power of AI! Learn to leverage cutting-edge tools for generating images, videos, presentations, and professional content that enhances your work.

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

  • Apply effective prompting strategies for generating high-quality visual and multimedia content
  • Create professional presentations, graphics, and marketing materials using AI tools
  • Understand the ethical and legal considerations of AI-generated media
  • Build a portfolio showcasing AI-enhanced professional media assets
