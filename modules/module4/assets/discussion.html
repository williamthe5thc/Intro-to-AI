Module 4 Discussion: Showcasing Your AI-Created Media

Share your AI-generated media creations, discuss your experiences with different tools, and learn from your peers' creative approaches.

Discussion Prompt: Your AI Media Creation Journey

Now that you've explored various AI media creation tools and completed the comparison matrix, it's time to share your experiences and creations with the class.

1Share Your Creation

Post a piece of media you created using AI tools. Describe what you made and which tool you used.

2Describe Your Process

Walk us through your creation process. What prompts did you use? How many iterations did it take?

3Share Lessons Learned

What surprised you? What challenges did you face? What tips would you share with others?

4Professional Applications

How could this type of content be used in your professional work? What value does it provide?

Types of Media to Share

Generated Images

Marketing graphics, illustrations, concept art, social media visuals

Presentations

Business slides, educational content, pitch decks, reports

Video Content

Training videos, explainers, social media clips, animations

Design Assets

Logos, icons, brochures, infographics, brand materials

Discussion Guidelines & Best Practices
  • Share Responsibly: Only post content you have rights to use and that's appropriate for professional settings
  • Give Credit: Always mention which AI tool you used to create your content
  • Be Constructive: Provide helpful feedback and ask thoughtful questions about others' work
  • Document Your Process: Include details about prompts, iterations, and techniques used
  • Discuss Professional Applications: Focus on how these tools can enhance professional work
  • Engage Actively: Comment meaningfully on at least 3 classmates' posts

Sample Posts for Inspiration

SM
Sarah Martinez • Marketing Director • Posted 3 hours ago

My Creation: I used DALL-E 3 to create a series of professional headshots for our company's "About Us" page when we couldn't arrange a photoshoot in time.

Process: My prompt was: "Professional corporate headshot of a confident marketing director, business attire, neutral background, LinkedIn style, high quality, professional lighting." It took about 5 iterations to get the style consistent across different team members.

Lessons Learned: Being specific about lighting and style helped maintain consistency. I was surprised by how realistic the results were, but I had to be careful about making them look too perfect.

Professional Application: This saved us $2,000+ in photography costs and allowed us to launch our website redesign on schedule. The AI-generated images actually perform better in our A/B tests than stock photos!

Tip for Others: Create a style guide prompt that you can reuse for consistency across multiple images.

DK
David Kim • Training Manager • Posted 5 hours ago

My Creation: Used Gamma to create an entire onboarding presentation for new hires. What usually takes me 8+ hours took less than 2 hours!

Process: I input our key onboarding topics and company information. Gamma generated slides, suggested layouts, and even recommended relevant stock images. I then customized the content and branding.

Biggest Surprise: The AI actually suggested better information flow than my usual approach. It organized content more logically than I initially planned.

Challenge: Had to spend time fact-checking and ensuring all company-specific information was accurate. AI got the general structure right but needed human oversight for details.

Professional Impact: This has revolutionized how quickly I can create training materials. I'm now able to focus more time on interactive activities rather than slide creation.

Reflection Questions for Your Post

Consider these questions to create a thoughtful, valuable discussion post:

  • How did the AI tool perform compared to your expectations?
  • What aspects of your professional workflow could be improved with AI media creation?
  • What quality standards do you need to maintain when using AI-generated content professionally?
  • How might clients or colleagues react to knowing content was AI-generated?
  • What ethical considerations should professionals keep in mind when using these tools?
  • Which tool features would you prioritize if you were purchasing for your organization?
