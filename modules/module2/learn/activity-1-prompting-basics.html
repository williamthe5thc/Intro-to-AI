Video Segments

Segment 1: What Makes a Good Prompt? (5 minutes)
  • The difference between casual conversation and effective prompting
  • Common prompting mistakes professionals make
  • The anatomy of a well-structured prompt
Segment 2: The CLEAR Framework (8 minutes)

Learn our proven framework for professional prompting:

  • Context: Setting the professional scenario
  • Length: Specifying desired output length
  • Examples: Providing relevant samples
  • Audience: Defining your target audience
  • Role: Establishing the AI's perspective
Segment 3: Before and After Examples (5 minutes)

See real workplace prompts transformed using CLEAR:

  • Email drafting prompts
  • Report summarization requests
  • Meeting agenda creation
  • Data analysis instructions

Key Takeaways

  1. Specificity beats generality - Vague prompts yield vague results
  2. Context is king - The more relevant context you provide, the better the output
  3. Iteration is normal - Professional prompting often requires refinement
  4. Role-playing works - Asking AI to adopt specific professional roles improves results
