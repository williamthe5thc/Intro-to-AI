The CLEAR Framework

Learn our proven method for professional prompting:

  • Context: Set the professional scenario and background
  • Length: Specify your desired output length and format
  • Examples: Provide relevant samples and references
  • Audience: Define who will use or see the output
  • Role: Establish the AI's perspective and expertise
