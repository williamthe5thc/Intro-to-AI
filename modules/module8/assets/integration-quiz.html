Healthcare System Case Study Expert Level

A 1,200-employee regional healthcare system wants to implement AI for clinical decision support and administrative efficiency. They have strong leadership support but face significant resistance from physicians concerned about patient safety and liability. Current technology infrastructure is adequate but not optimized. Budget allocation is substantial ($2M over 3 years).

Key Constraints: Regulatory compliance requirements (HIPAA, FDA), physician autonomy concerns, patient safety mandates, and complex integration with existing electronic health records.
