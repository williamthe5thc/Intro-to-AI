Module 3: Chatbots in Legal Context

Explore how AI-powered chatbots are transforming legal services, from client intake to document review, while navigating the critical ethical and practical considerations.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Understand how chatbots function in legal practice environments
  • Evaluate no-code chatbot platforms for legal applications
  • Analyze the ethical and legal implications of chatbot deployment
  • Design appropriate use cases for legal chatbots
  • Recognize limitations and risks in legal AI applications
