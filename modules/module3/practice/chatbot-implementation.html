Chatbot Implementation Case Study

Apply your knowledge of chatbot fundamentals, no-code platforms, and legal/ethical considerations to design and evaluate a comprehensive chatbot implementation strategy.

Practice Objectives

Duration: 45-60 minutes
Format: Individual case analysis
Submission: Written report + presentation
Assessment: Comprehensive rubric

Case Scenario: Peterson & Associates Law Firm

Firm Background: Peterson & Associates is a mid-sized law firm (25 attorneys) specializing in corporate law, employment law, and intellectual property. They receive approximately 200 initial client inquiries per month through their website.

Current Challenge: The firm is overwhelmed with initial client intake calls and website inquiries. Many potential clients abandon the process due to long response times (averaging 48-72 hours). The managing partner wants to implement a chatbot to improve response times and capture more qualified leads.

Key Stakeholders:

  • Sarah Peterson - Managing Partner (wants efficiency but concerned about ethics)
  • Michael Chen - IT Director (advocates for advanced AI solutions)
  • Dr. Lisa Martinez - Ethics Committee Chair (prioritizes compliance)
  • James Wilson - Client Relations Manager (wants better client experience)

Your Role: You are a legal technology consultant hired to evaluate chatbot options and provide implementation recommendations that balance efficiency, ethics, and legal compliance.

Case Analysis Tasks

Task 1: Needs Assessment & Use Case Definition

Analyze the firm's requirements and define appropriate chatbot use cases.

Consider: What specific tasks should the chatbot handle? What should it avoid? How does this align with the firm's practice areas and client types?
Your Analysis Should Include:
  • Identification of 3-5 specific chatbot use cases appropriate for this firm
  • Clear boundaries of what the chatbot should NOT do
  • Analysis of how different practice areas might benefit differently
  • Consideration of client demographics and technology comfort levels
Task 2: Platform Evaluation

Evaluate and compare no-code chatbot platforms for this specific implementation.

Consider: Which platforms best meet the firm's needs? What are the key evaluation criteria? How do costs compare to benefits?
Your Evaluation Should Include:
  • Comparison of at least 3 no-code chatbot platforms
  • Evaluation criteria matrix (features, cost, security, compliance)
  • Recommendation with clear justification
  • Implementation timeline and resource requirements
Task 3: Legal & Ethical Compliance Analysis

Address legal and ethical considerations for the proposed chatbot implementation.

Consider: How will you ensure compliance with professional ethics rules? What disclaimers and safeguards are needed? How will you protect client confidentiality?
Your Compliance Analysis Should Include:
  • Identification of relevant ethics rules and potential violations
  • Required disclaimers and user notifications
  • Data security and confidentiality measures
  • Ongoing monitoring and compliance procedures
  • Risk mitigation strategies for identified concerns
Task 4: Stakeholder Presentation

Prepare recommendations for presentation to firm leadership.

Consider: How will you address each stakeholder's concerns? What implementation approach will gain buy-in? How will you measure success?
Your Presentation Should Include:
  • Executive summary with clear recommendations
  • Addressing specific stakeholder concerns
  • Implementation roadmap with phases and milestones
  • Budget analysis and ROI projections
  • Success metrics and monitoring plan

Required Deliverables

Written Report

5-7 page comprehensive analysis covering all four tasks with supporting research and citations.

  • Executive summary
  • Detailed task analyses
  • Platform comparison matrix
  • Compliance checklist
  • Implementation timeline

Slide Presentation

10-12 slide presentation suitable for firm partners and stakeholders.

  • Problem statement
  • Recommended solution
  • Platform comparison
  • Compliance measures
  • Implementation plan
  • Budget and ROI

Compliance Framework

Detailed compliance checklist and monitoring procedures.

  • Pre-launch checklist
  • Ongoing monitoring
  • Staff training plan
  • Client disclosure templates
  • Incident response plan

Evaluation Rubric

Criterion Excellent (4) Good (3) Satisfactory (2) Needs Improvement (1)
Needs Assessment Comprehensive analysis with innovative insights Thorough analysis of most key factors Basic analysis covering main points Incomplete or superficial analysis
Platform Evaluation Detailed comparison with clear methodology Good comparison with justification Basic comparison of platforms Limited or unclear comparison
Legal/Ethical Analysis Sophisticated understanding of all issues Good grasp of major legal/ethical concerns Basic understanding of key issues Minimal or incorrect legal analysis
Implementation Plan Realistic, detailed, and well-sequenced Good plan with clear phases Basic plan with some details Vague or unrealistic plan
Professional Presentation Polished, engaging, and persuasive Well-organized and professional Clear and adequately presented Poor organization or presentation

Success Tips

  • Research Real Platforms: Investigate actual no-code chatbot services for accurate comparisons
  • Cite Ethics Rules: Reference specific ABA Model Rules and state bar guidance
  • Think Like a Consultant: Consider practical implementation challenges and costs
  • Address All Stakeholders: Show how your solution meets different stakeholder needs
  • Be Specific: Provide concrete examples and actionable recommendations
  • Consider Edge Cases: Think about what could go wrong and how to prevent it
