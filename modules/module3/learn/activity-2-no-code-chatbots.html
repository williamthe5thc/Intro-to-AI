Learning Activity 2: Assessing No-Code Chatbot Platforms

Learn how to evaluate and select no-code chatbot platforms for professional applications. Discover the tools that make chatbot creation accessible to non-technical professionals.

Learning Objectives

After completing this activity, you will be able to:

  • Identify key features to look for in no-code chatbot platforms
  • Compare popular no-code platforms and their capabilities
  • Assess platform suitability for specific professional use cases
  • Understand pricing models and scalability considerations
  • Evaluate integration options with existing business systems
